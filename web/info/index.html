Hey! We're furryli.st - BlueSky's first furry feed! We collate furry content from across BlueSky and present it to you in an easy to consume manner. We're hoping to grow over time to provide services related to furry social media.

Right now, BlueSky doesn't support using custom feeds, but once that's supported you'll be able to view our feed in app. If you'd like to try us out before then, you can use SkyFeed.

You'll find more up-to-date news on our lead maintainers BlueSky feed.
